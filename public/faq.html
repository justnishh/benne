FAQs

Common questions about our restaurant, website, and your data

Restaurant

What are your opening hours?

Bandra West: Tue–Sat 7 AM – 3:30 PM & 5 – 11:30 PM. Sunday 7 AM – 4 PM & 5 – 11:30 PM. Monday closed.
Juhu: Check @benne.bombay on Instagram for latest timings.

Where are you located?

Bandra West: Shop no. 1, Plot 85, TPS 3, Louis Bell Building, 16th Rd, opp. Shree Sagar, Bandra West, Mumbai 400050.
Juhu: Ground floor, Nirav Apartment, 1, Gulmohar Rd, next to IDFC First Bank, Juhu, Mumbai 400049.

Is the food 100% vegetarian?

Yes. Benne is a fully vegetarian restaurant. We do not serve eggs, meat, or seafood — just like the darshinis in Bangalore that inspired us.

Do you take reservations?

No. We operate on a walk-in basis only, in the true darshini style. There are standing tables and no chairs — it's quick, authentic, and part of the experience.

What is a "darshini"?

A darshini is a type of quick-service vegetarian restaurant common in Bangalore. Standing tables, self-service, no frills — just great food served fast. We've brought that same format to Mumbai.

Do you offer takeaway or delivery?

We primarily focus on dine-in. Takeaway availability may vary by location. We are also listed on Zomato — check there for delivery options.

How much does a meal cost?

An average meal costs ₹200–400 per person. Our dosas start from ₹100 and filter coffee is ₹40.

Data & Privacy

What personal data do you collect?

Only what you voluntarily provide through our contact form: your name, email, subject, and message. We also collect basic server logs (IP address, browser type). That's it.

Do you sell my data to third parties?

No. Never. Your data is confidential and will never be sold, rented, or shared with any third party for marketing or commercial purposes. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.

How can I get my data deleted?

Email us at hello@shimowfoods.com with your request. Under the DPDP Act 2023, you have the right to request erasure of your personal data. We'll process your request within 30 days.

How long do you keep my data?

Contact form submissions are retained for up to 12 months, then permanently deleted. You can request earlier deletion at any time.

Website

Does this website use cookies?

We don't use first-party tracking cookies. However, our Contact page embeds Google Maps, which may set third-party cookies under Google's Privacy Policy.

Are there third-party links on this site?

Yes — we link to Google Maps, Instagram, Zomato, and WhatsApp. These are governed by their own terms and privacy policies. We are not responsible for their content or practices.

Who operates this website?

This website is operated by Shimow Hospitality Pvt. Ltd., the company behind Benne. For any queries, email hello@shimowfoods.com.
