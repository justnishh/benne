Privacy Policy

How we handle your data — transparently and responsibly

Effective date: 2 March 2026
Last updated: 2 March 2026

Benne is operated by Shimow Hospitality Pvt. Ltd. ("we", "us", "our"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data in compliance with the Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023 and the Information Technology Act, 2000 of India.

1. Data We Collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary to operate our website and respond to your enquiries:

  • Contact form submissions: name, email address, subject, and message content
  • Automatically collected: IP address, browser type, and pages visited (via server logs)
  • Third-party embeds: Google Maps on our Contact page may set cookies — see Section 5

We do not collect payment information, Aadhaar numbers, or any sensitive personal data through this website.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To respond to enquiries submitted through our contact form
  • To improve website functionality and user experience
  • To comply with legal obligations

We process your data only for the specific purpose for which it was collected, in accordance with the purpose limitation principle under the DPDP Act.

3. We Never Sell Your Data

Your personal data is confidential and will never be sold, rented, traded, or shared with any third party for marketing or commercial purposes. This is a firm commitment — no exceptions.

We may share data only when required by law, court order, or regulatory authority.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

Contact form submissions are retained for up to 12 months to allow us to follow up on your enquiry. After this period, data is permanently deleted.

You may request earlier deletion at any time — see Section 6.

5. Cookies & Third-Party Services

This website does not use first-party tracking cookies. However, embedded Google Maps iframes on our Contact page may set third-party cookies governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Your Rights Under the DPDP Act

As a data principal, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a summary of your personal data we hold
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent — withdraw consent for data processing at any time
  • Grievance redressal — raise a complaint if you believe your data has been mishandled

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data from unauthorised access, alteration, or destruction. Our website is served over HTTPS to encrypt data in transit.

8. Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

9. Contact for Data Requests

For any data-related requests, questions, or grievances, contact us at:

We will respond to all data requests within 30 days.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
